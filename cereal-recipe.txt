Ingredients needed:
Milk
Favorite cereal

Supplies needed:
Bowl
Spoon

Steps:
1.) Add desired amount of cereal to the bowl. Make sure to leave room for the milk.
2.) Add desired amount of milk to the cereal in bowl. Preferrably enough to saturate all of the cereal.
3.)Optional: Stir milk and cereal together.

NOTE: DO NOT POUR MILK FIRST THEN CEREAL LAST. THIS IS NOT NORMAL AND SHOULD NEVER BE DONE.
